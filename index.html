Smooths On Top

In the pursuit of a healthier lifestyle, we often come across options that don’t always meet our expectations. That’s why, in our project Healthy Smooths, we’ve decided to launch an innovative proposal: to offer natural smoothies, full of nutrients and fresh flavors, perfect for improving our health and well-being.

More information

Logo Concept: Natural, Simple, Smooth

At Smooths on Top, we aim to attract our customers with an innovative proposal that combines the natural with the minimalist. We are inspired by the simplicity and freshness of natural ingredients, fused with a clean and modern design, creating a unique experience that highlights the best of both worlds. This is how Smooths on Top was born, where each smoothie is a celebration of the simple and delicious.

The Different Types of Smooths

Currently, at Healthy Smooths, we offer three delicious types of smoothies:
the classic Banana with Milk, the refreshing Strawberry with Milk, and our exclusive limited edition Bluemace, a unique blend you won't want to miss. Each of these smoothies is made with natural ingredients and designed to provide you with a refreshing and healthy taste experience.

Monkey Madness-Banana

Smooth, nutritious, and full of energy. The Banana with Milk is the perfect combination of creaminess and natural sweetness, providing potassium, fiber, and a delicious taste in every sip. Ideal for starting the day with a great mood.

More information

Pinkywinky-Strawberry

Refreshing, creamy, and with the perfect touch of natural sweetness. Our Strawberry with Milk is made with fresh strawberries and high-quality milk, offering you a drink full of vitamin C and an irresistible taste. A classic that never fails!

Bluemace (Limited Edition)

Our limited edition Bluemace is a fun tribute to McDonald's character, Grimace, and is made with the finest blueberries, known for their antioxidant properties and unique flavor. This smoothie combines the natural sweetness of blueberries with a smooth and refreshing texture, offering a delicious experience that you can only enjoy for a limited time. Don’t miss the chance to try it!

Desserts

At Healthy Smooths, we not only offer delicious smoothies, but we also bring you fresh and healthy desserts. Made with the highest quality ingredients, they are the perfect complement to pair with your favorite smoothie. Enjoy our chocolate chip cookies, brownies, and vanilla ice cream — the ideal sweet touch for any moment. Because something delicious can also be natural!

Chocolate Cookies

Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, with melted chocolate chunks in every bite. Our chocolate cookies are the perfect snack to accompany your smoothie or treat yourself to a sweet indulgence without overdoing it.

Brownies

An irresistible classic: soft, fluffy, and with the perfect balance between sweetness and the rich intensity of chocolate. Our brownies are made with quality ingredients to bring you a delicious dessert full of flavor.

Ice Cream

Creamy, refreshing, and with the authentic taste of natural vanilla. Our ice cream is the perfect touch to sweeten your day while still enjoying a delicious and light option.
